# Independent Verification Guide - Original 185-Curve Validation

> **Note**: This verification list corresponds to the **original 185-curve validation** with detailed statistical analysis (p = 1.29 × 10⁻⁴², cross-validation, clustering metrics). For the scaled 975-curve validation, see [README.md](README.md) and [FINAL_975_LMFDB_VALIDATION.json](FINAL_975_LMFDB_VALIDATION.json). This document enables independent verification of the original validation results.

This document provides the complete list of all 185 elliptic curves used in the original ALYCON validation, with direct links to verify each curve's CM status on the LMFDB (L-functions and Modular Forms Database).

## Dataset Overview

- **Total Curves**: 185
- **CM Curves**: 83 (curves with Complex Multiplication)
- **Non-CM Curves**: 102 (curves without Complex Multiplication)

## How to Verify

Each curve label below links directly to its LMFDB page where you can independently verify:
1. The curve's official CM discriminant (if it has CM)
2. Whether the curve has complex multiplication or not
3. Other properties (conductor, rank, torsion, j-invariant)

**LMFDB is the authoritative source** - it's maintained by the number theory research community and all data is peer-reviewed.
